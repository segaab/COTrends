About the Dashboard

This dashboard watches sectors for incoming growth. It tracks the performance of the sector leader and sees how the rest of the sector follows. When the leader pulls ahead, it’s a sign that new capital is entering the market. The rest of the sector usually confirms this trend as the momentum spreads.

Key Features

  • Leader-Follower Tracking: See which stocks are leading a sector and which are following behind.
  • Negative Space Monitoring: Measure the gap between the leader and the followers to spot early growth signals.
  • Phase Detection: Identify early, mid, and late growth stages in a sector.
  • Visual Dashboard: Interactive charts that make it easy to understand sector dynamics.
  • Backtesting: Test how your strategy would have performed in the past.

Who It’s For

The dashboard is built for retail investors, especially those aged 25–35, who want to spot growth early, follow market leaders, and make more informed trading decisions.

How It Works

We track normalized stock prices to see how a leader performs versus its peers. When the leader pulls ahead, it signals a potential sector growth wave. As other stocks start to follow, the dashboard confirms the wave and highlights the phase the sector is in. This makes it easier to spot growth opportunities and potential interruptions before they happen.
