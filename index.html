About the Dashboard

The Sector Wave Dashboard is a professional tool designed for retail investors seeking actionable insights into sector movements. Using a normalized analysis of leading stocks versus their peers, it identifies early signals of sector growth and potential interruptions, helping you make data-driven decisions.

Key Features

  • Leader-Follower Monitoring: Track sector leaders and the convergence of followers in real-time.
  • Negative Space Analysis: Quantify the performance gap between leading and following stocks to spot early inflection points.
  • Inflection Phase Detection: Identify early, mid, and late growth phases to inform entry and exit decisions.
  • Visual Dashboard: Interactive charts with normalized performance, negative space, and portfolio impact.
  • Backtesting Capability: Test how signals would have performed historically to validate your strategy.
  • Alerts: Get notified of early sector growth and potential profit-taking moments.

How It Works

The dashboard monitors normalized stock performance within a sector, calculating the gap between a leading stock and its peers. It tracks how that gap shrinks or expands over time, indicating early waves of growth or potential interruptions. While the analysis is grounded in advanced quantitative metrics, the interface presents insights clearly and professionally, without requiring a background in data science.
